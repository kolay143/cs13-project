About Bukidnon

Bukidnon is a landlocked province in the Philippines known for its cool climate, lush landscapes, and rich cultural heritage. It offers a perfect blend of nature, tradition, and adventure for travelers seeking a unique experience.

Top Attractions

Mount Kitanglad

Explore breathtaking hiking trails and panoramic views in this majestic mountain range.

Lake Apo

Relax by the serene waters of this hidden volcanic crater lake surrounded by lush forests.

Kaamulan Festival

Experience the vibrant cultural festival celebrating the indigenous peoples of Bukidnon.
